About Us

Sun NXT

South Indian streaming service offering movies, TV shows, and originals in Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, and Kannada. It features a diverse range of regional content, including popular TV serials, movies, and exclusive Sun NXT originals.

Disney+ Hotstar

Disney+ Hotstar blends the magic of Disney with a vast range of entertainment. Featuring Disney, Marvel, and Star Wars content, it caters to diverse tastes, offering a mix of regional and international shows, movies, and sports events.

JioCinema

JioCinema, part of the Jio ecosystem, provides a vast library of movies, TV shows, and original content. It caters to Jio subscribers, offering a seamless streaming experience with a diverse content catalog.

MX Player

MX Player, originally a video player, has evolved into a full-fledged OTT platform. It hosts a diverse range of content, including movies, web series, and TV shows, appealing to a broad audience with its free and premium offerings.

Netflix

Netflix is a global streaming giant, offering an extensive library of high-quality TV shows, movies, and documentaries. Renowned for original content, it pioneers innovative storytelling across genres, captivating audiences with compelling narratives and stellar production values

Amazon Prime Video

Amazon Prime Video, part of the e-commerce behemoth, delivers a diverse array of content, including original series, films, and exclusive shows. Combining entertainment with perks like free shipping, it ensures a seamless viewing experience for subscribers seeking quality and convenience.

SonyLIV

SonyLIV, by Sony Pictures Networks, provides a rich assortment of content, spanning live TV, original series, and a plethora of movies. With a focus on quality, it ensures a delightful viewing experience for discerning audiences.

Aha

Zee5 is a comprehensive platform featuring a blend of Zee network content, original series, and a diverse range of movies. Catering to various languages and genres, it appeals to a broad audience with its entertainment offerings.
